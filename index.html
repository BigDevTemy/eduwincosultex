WHO WE ARE @ EDUWINCONSULTEX

At Eduwin consultex, we give you professional topmost support and soft-landing in your dreamed and studying destination. We don’t just give you support in your journey to study abroad; we give you success and joy. We are the only Educational consultant who do what others don’t do very well.
Studying abroad is more than just an academic experience — it’s a life experience. We have a wide range of services and support programme for our esteemed clients.
University admissions process can be very stressful and confusing, especially for students located outside of the countries of interest. Access to accurate information and expert guidance help families and students face the rigors of applications more informed and better equipped to find perfect institution with competitive pricing.

Our Services

School Placement.

Eduwin Consultex is a unique educational consultancy that helps students in schools and universities placement across the world including UK, USA and Canada..

Study Recommendations.

Eudwin provides supports for students as they start their international education journey around the world. We guide you through each step of the process and assist whenever you need us. We ensure that you get things right first time, using our expertise and experience to help you avoid any pitfalls along the way.

School Guidance Services.

We provide a guidance service that helps you to explore all of your options for international education. We use a range of support tools to help us understand what you’re looking for and to help you make the right choice for your studies.

Admimission Processing.

Once you have decided where you would like to study, the next step is to arrange admission to your chosen school or university. This part of the process is fully supported by our academic support counsellors, who partners all over the world and are specialists in school and university admissions.

Visa Guidance.

If your destination country requires you to secure a visa for your travel and studies, our support team will work with you to complete your visa application.

Pre-departure And Travel Briefings.

Our education support team will continue to guide you through to your departure date, organising briefings and consultations along the way to discuss any questions or concerns you may have.

Long Term Support.

At EDUWIN, we superior in communication. We will stay in touch to support your journey throughout the course of your studies, ensuring to be on hand with advice and assistance whenever you need us.

Corporate Training.

Eduwin Consultex as one of the world’s leading educational consultant and human capital development company. Is your school, company, organization, institution, or government agencies looking to commence corporate training overseas, we can help to connect to world-class Schools and Universities.

Human Capital Development.

At Eduwin, our team of HR Professional assist in business development, manpower development, recruitment, outsourcing, onboarding and performance management. .
